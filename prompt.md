Implement a Culinary Studio Operations & Recipe Coach Platform for an on-premise cooking school that sells memberships, schedules classes, and guides members through recipes in a hands-free kitchen environment. The Svelte web interface provides separate workspaces for Members, Instructors, Front Desk Staff, and Administrators. Members can browse offerings such as monthly memberships, single-seat group classes, personal coaching sessions, and value-added services, then book a time slot, join a waitlist when a class is full, reschedule within policy, or request cancellation with clear fee previews before confirming. During a cooking session, a member can select any recipe and generate a one-click executable workflow that breaks it into timed steps with parallel and sequential tasks, built-in waits (preheat, resting, marinating), and temperature/heat cues; the UI runs a unified timeline with automatic timers, supports step rollback to the prior completed step, and offers spoken step readouts using the device’s local text-to-speech engine plus large “Next/Back/Pause” controls for flour-safe operation. The in-app Notification Center shows templated messages for booking success, schedule changes, cancellations, waitlist promotion, and class reminders, with per-user mute controls and a history screen showing what was delivered and when. Staff dashboards provide growth reporting tailored to culinary learning, including recipe view volume, weekly consistency streaks, difficulty progression, completion accuracy (timers completed vs. skipped/rolled back), and interest distribution by cuisine tags, with drill-down by date range and permissioned export to a local file.

The backend uses Fastify to implement a decoupled REST API consumed by the Svelte frontend, persisting all data locally in PostgreSQL for fully offline operation on a single machine or a LAN. The product and billing engine models price books with version management so new pricing takes effect on a configured date without altering past invoices; it supports time-based access (e.g., $129.00/month), usage packs (e.g., 10 class credits expiring after 180 days), and amount-based stored value (e.g., $250.00 kitchen wallet), with automatic receivable calculation including configurable sales tax (default 8.875%) and discounts capped at 30% unless an Administrator overrides with a reason. Because internet payments are out of scope, the system records tender types such as cash/check/manual card entry and tracks outstanding balances with due dates. Cancellation/reschedule automation enforces conflict validation (no overlapping bookings), renewal priority and quotas (e.g., members can renew or rebook 24 hours earlier than non-members), and tiered penalties such as free cancellation up to 24 hours prior, 50% charge within 24 hours, and 100% within 2 hours; released seats automatically backfill from the waitlist and generate in-app notifications. Security and privacy are enforced through explicit consent capture, data minimization defaults, encryption of sensitive fields at rest (e.g., AES-256 for notes and contact data) and TLS for in-transit connections even on LAN, password hashing with Argon2id, signed API requests with timestamps, idempotency keys for booking/billing actions, rate limiting (e.g., 60 requests/minute/user), immutable audit logs, and basic account risk controls such as lockout after 10 failed logins for 15 minutes. Open API and webhook callbacks are available for local integrations like door controllers or on-site BI displays, with versioned endpoints and retry queues that log failures and raise visible admin alerts until acknowledged.
